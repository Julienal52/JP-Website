How Pollution Impacts the Environment

Air Pollution

Vehicle fumes, industrial smoke, and burning waste fill our air with harmful gases that affect both humans and wildlife.

Water Pollution

Plastic waste, chemicals, and oil spills are poisoning rivers, lakes, and oceans, destroying marine life.

Deforestation

Cutting down trees for land and resources causes habitat loss, climate change, and soil erosion.

What We Can Do

Recycle & Reuse

Reduce waste by recycling plastics, glass, and metals, and reusing materials whenever possible.

Plant More Trees

Trees clean the air, provide oxygen, and support wildlife. Planting even one tree makes a difference.

Use Clean Energy

Switch to solar, wind, or hydro power to cut down reliance on fossil fuels and reduce greenhouse gases.
